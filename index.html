What You Should Include in Your Website Homepage Design 1. Headline Within three seconds, a website needs to tell visitors what the business has to offer. That's where your headline comes in. It may only be a few words, but it's one of the most important pieces of copy on your website. Many types of people might visit your website, and you'll be hard-pressed to find a few words that hit home for everyone. Instead, write your headline to target a third of those people who are most likely to be happy with your product.
